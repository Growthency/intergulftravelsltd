# Inter Gulf Travels Ltd

A world-class website and management platform for **Inter Gulf Travels Ltd** — a
government-licensed Hajj & Umrah travel agency in Dhaka, Bangladesh (Hajj License
No. 071, serving pilgrims since 2002).

## Highlights

- **Cinematic marketing site** — parallax aurora backgrounds, typewriter + shimmer
  hero, curved section dividers, buttery Lenis smooth-scroll, scroll-progress bar,
  custom gradient scrollbar, animated reveals — fully responsive on mobile, tablet
  and desktop.
- **Complete, real content** — Hajj & Umrah packages, guides, FAQs, services
  (visa, air ticket, hotels, tours), gallery, about suite, blog, contact & free
  estimate — no placeholder copy.
- **Member dashboard** with a secure **Document Vault** (passport, visa, ticket
  storage in private Supabase storage with signed-URL access).
- **Admin dashboard** — blog CMS, media library, gallery, navigation & footer
  builder, site settings, contact & estimate inbox, user management.
- **WebP-only image pipeline** — every uploaded image is auto-converted to
  optimized WebP server-side; a script converts local assets too.
- **Custom staff login URL** — admins sign in at a private slug (e.g. `/taslima`).
- Brand system derived from a custom **emerald + gold** logo; the whole UI,
  typography and buttons are built on it.
